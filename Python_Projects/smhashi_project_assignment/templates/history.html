Our History

Waamo Restaurant was founded with the mission to bring the rich and flavorful traditions of Somali and Mediterranean cuisine to our community. Named after the historic Waamo region, the restaurant embodies the warmth, hospitality, and diverse flavors of Somali culture. Since opening our doors, Waamo has become a gathering place for families and friends, offering authentic dishes prepared with the finest ingredients and time-honored recipes. From hearty entrees like Hilib and Chicken Sukhaar to savory appetizers like Sambusa and Hummus Dip, our menu reflects a commitment to culinary excellence and cultural pride. Join us and experience a taste of Somalia, right in the heart of your neighborhood.
